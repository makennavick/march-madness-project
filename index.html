Offensive/Defensive Analyses

OFFENSIVE METRICS

This graph shows key offensive metrics for teams that made it to each round.

  • adjOE does significantly increase for teams that made it further.
  • Turnover Rate (TOR) doesn’t change much
  • You can see a slight but steady increase in Effective Field Goal percentage (EFG%) and 2 point percentage (2P%) increase showing improved shot quality and execution.
  • Free throw rate (FTR) and 3 point percentage (3P%) don’t seem to be a primary differentiator for teams making a deep run.
  • Offensive Rebound (OFB) does show a slight upward trend suggesting teams are better at getting better at getting those rebounds offensively.

OFFENSIVE CORRELATION

  • EFG% (Effective Field Goal %) has the strongest positive correlation.
  • TOR (Turnover Rate) has a strong negative correlation — fewer turnovers = better offense.
  • Other metrics like 2P%, 3P%, and ORB show moderate positive influence

DEFENSIVE METRICS

  • AdjDE drops for teams who made it further in the tournament — meaning defensive efficiency gets better.
  • EFGD%, 2P%D, and 3P%D all drop steadily — meaning elite teams defend both inside and outside shots effectively.
  • TORD (Turnover Rate Forced) stays relatively stable

DEFENSIVE CORRELATION

  • EFGD% (Effective Field Goal % Defense) has the strongest positive correlation. (0.68)
  • 2P%D (Opponent 2PT FG%) has a strong correlation (0.63) — Interior defense (rim protection) is critical to team defense
  • 3P%D (Opponent 3PT FG% has a moderate correlation (0.41) — Defending the arc isn’t as important as the paint and overall shot quality)

OFFENSIVE VS DEFENSIVE

  • While top-tier defense is a common characteristic of deep tournament teams, elite offensive efficiency often serves as the differentiating factor among championship contenders.
  • Strong defense is a baseline requirement for tournament success, but elite offense may ultimately determine how far a team advances.
